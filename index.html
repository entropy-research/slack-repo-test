Amazing Penguin Facts

Emperor Penguins

The largest penguin species, standing up to 4 feet tall and weighing up to 88 pounds!

Penguin Diet

Penguins primarily eat fish, squid, and krill, consuming up to 2 pounds of food per day.

Swimming Speed

Penguins can swim at speeds up to 22 mph, making them incredibly fast underwater!
